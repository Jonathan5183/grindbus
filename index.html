Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a ticket?

You can book a ticket by entering your origin, destination, date, and number of passengers in the booking form on the home page. Then, select your preferred bus and complete the secure payment process.

Can I track my bus in real-time?

Yes, GrindBus offers live tracking for all journeys. Once your booking is confirmed, you will receive details on how to track your bus in real-time.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and popular digital wallets for secure and convenient payments.

How can I cancel or modify my booking?

You can manage your bookings through your account dashboard. Please refer to our cancellation and modification policy for details on applicable charges and timelines.
